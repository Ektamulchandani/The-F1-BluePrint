The F-1 Blueprint
The F-1 Blueprint is a free, open-source tool for international students navigating the United States — from the moment they decide to apply, to the moment they sign a full-time offer.
It was built by an international student who went through every step of this process: applying from abroad, arriving with no US network, landing internships on CPT, converting to full-time on OPT, and now navigating H-1B sponsorship. No institution funded this. No consulting firm built it. It exists because no single resource like it existed when it was needed most.
What it covers:
Getting into a US program, F-1 visa and pre-arrival preparation, first semester strategy, internship hunting on CPT, full-time offer conversion on OPT, and H-1B sponsorship targeting — all anchored to real timelines and real decisions.
Who it is for:
International students at the master's level who want to study and work in the US and need a clear, honest, step-by-step guide from someone who has done it.
How to contribute:
If you have been through this journey — as a student, a DSO, a recruiter, or an employer — your knowledge belongs here. See CONTRIBUTING.md to add steps, correct information, or build on what exists.
